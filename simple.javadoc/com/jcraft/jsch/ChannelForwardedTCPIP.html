public class ChannelForwardedTCPIP
extends Channel

A Channel created when a forwarded port at the remote side is connected. This is used internally to forward remote ports to servers at the local side or to local daemons.

See Also:
Session.setPortForwardingR(String, int, String, int, SocketFactory), Session.setPortForwardingR(String, int, String, Object[]), RFC 4254, section 7.2 TCP/IP Forwarding Channels
